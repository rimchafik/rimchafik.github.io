About PawsFinder

PawsFinder was inspired by my deep love for animals and my personal experience of having four fur babies whom I adore. As a pet owner, I've always been concerned about their well-being and safety, which motivated me to create a platform that not only helps connect pets with loving homes but also aids in finding lost pets and ensuring their safe return.

This project is a Portfolio Project for ALX, where I am developing my skills as a web developer. Learn more about ALX and its programs here.
