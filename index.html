Key Features

Feature 1: Pet Matching

Find your ideal pet companion based on your preferences such as breed, age, and size.

Feature 2: Adoption Alerts

Receive real-time alerts about newly available pets that match your saved preferences.

Feature 3: Pet Profiles

View detailed profiles of pets including photos, characteristics, and adoption information.

About PawsFinder

PawsFinder was inspired by my passion for connecting animals in need with loving homes. As a lifelong animal lover, I wanted to create a platform that simplifies the pet adoption process and helps potential pet owners find their perfect match.
